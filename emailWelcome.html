Thanks for signing up to be a Food Donor, and welcome to the Medford Food Project! We've been really busy with our most recent food collection, which took place last Saturday. Good news:  On April 14th, we picked up 21,000 lbs. of food in the Medford area…and 23 tons in the whole county. Next time, with your help, we hope to collect even more!


Just to remind you, our program is really simple. Every week when you go to the supermarket (starting this week!), buy an extra item or two. Store the extra food in your green MFP bag…and on a the 2nd Saturday of every even month  (Feb, April, June. Aug. Oct, and Dec.), one of your neighbors will come by to pick it up. You don't even have to be there -- you can leave it out for them. Don't worry about forgetting the date: we'll send you a reminder e-mail a week before the pickup, then again a day before. All you'll have to do is put the food outside your front door.  Our next pickup is June 9.

Your participation really matters.  This year, more than 30,000 Jackson County residents will need emergency supplies of food -- a whopping 15% of the population!  It's very sad. But that's why your willingness to be part of this great program is so wonderful. Our neighbors need help -- and working together we can do something about it. This is the true meaning of community. When each of us pitches in a little, we can accomplish a lot.

The next step:  Your Neighborhood Coordinator will be in touch with you soon.  If you don't already have one of our green Medford Food Project bags to use for food storage, they'll bring you one. 

If you have any questions or suggestions about the MFP, or need to communicate for any reason, please get in touch. We can be reached at  foodproject@opendoor.com , or at 541-210-8288.

Again, thanks so much for helping to make this happen.  No other county in America has regular door-to-door food collection   like we do in Jackson County. And it's really making a difference! 

We look forward to working with you.
Highest regards,
